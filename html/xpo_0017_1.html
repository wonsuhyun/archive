About Xportsmedia

Korea No.1 Entertainment and Sports News

No.1 online media you can trust

Xportsmedia was founded in 2007, starting off with Korea’s number one entertainment and sports media, Xportsnews.
It expanded its content coverage to e-sports and game, fashion and beauty, lifestyle, and more and is constantly growing.

In 2018, it launched “Daily Smart,” a media specializing in economy, and is leaping forward as an online media conglomerate.
